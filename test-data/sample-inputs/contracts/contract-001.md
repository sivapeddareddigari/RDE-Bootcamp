## 1. Parties

- **Client:** *Northstar Civic Group, Inc.* (a fictional entity created for this case)
- **Service Provider:** Meridian Atlas Partners

## 2. Scope of Services

The Service Provider will deliver the Phase 2 feasibility study, including site survey support, environmental review coordination, stakeholder workshops, and final reporting.

## 3. Fees and Rate Table

Time charged at the following standard role rates (USD per hour). Rates apply to all approved time logged against the project unless overridden in writing by the Project Lead.

| Role code | Role | Rate (USD/hr) |
|---|---|---|
| ENG1 | Engineer I | 145 |
| ENG2 | Engineer II | 175 |
| ENG3 | Senior Engineer | 230 |
| PM1 | Project Manager | 215 |
| PRIN | Principal | 320 |
| ADMIN | Admin / Coordinator | 95 |

Travel time is billable at 50% of the role rate, capped at 8 hours per traveller per direction.

Subcontractor pass-through is permitted at cost plus 8%.

## 4. Reimbursable Expenses

Reimbursable at cost, subject to receipts and the limits below.

| Category | Limit / Rule |
|---|---|
| Air travel | Economy class for flights under 6 hours; premium economy permitted for flights ≥ 6 hours. |
| Lodging | Up to USD 275/night in major metros, USD 195/night elsewhere. |
| Ground transport | Reasonable taxi, rideshare, public transit, or rental compact class. |
| Meals (with receipt) | Up to USD 90/day per traveller. |
| Per diem (no receipts) | USD 65/day in lieu of meal receipts, at traveller's election; not stackable with category above. |
| Mileage | At the prevailing federal mileage rate, no receipt required, mileage log required. |
| Telephony / data | Reimbursable when project-specific. |
| Alcohol | **Not reimbursable** under any circumstance. |
| Personal items | Not reimbursable. |
| Entertainment | Not reimbursable without prior written approval from the Project Lead. |

Receipts are required for all expenses above USD 25 unless the category explicitly waives them (mileage, per diem).

## 5. Invoicing

Invoices are issued monthly, within fifteen business days of month-end. Each invoice will include:

- A summary of labour by role and total hours
- A summary of reimbursable expenses by category
- Backup documentation for all reimbursable expenses (excluding mileage and per diem)
- Any subcontractor invoices, with markup line shown separately

Invoice classification code in the Service Provider's SAP shall be set to `STD-TM-NA-CIVIC`.

## 6. Approval & Payment

Each invoice must be reviewed and approved in writing by the Client's authorised approver (currently the **Project Lead** of record at Meridian, on behalf of the Client per the project governance plan) prior to release. Payment terms net 45 days from approved-invoice date.

## 7. Tax

US sales tax does not apply to professional services in the engagement jurisdiction. Where any tax becomes applicable due to a change in law or scope, the Service Provider will notify the Client and apply tax in accordance with applicable law.

## 8. Holds and Adjustments

The Project Lead may place a hold on any line or transaction by notifying the Service Provider in writing. Held items will not be billed in the current cycle and will be reconsidered in the following cycle.

## 9. Disputes

Either party may dispute any line item within ninety days of the invoice date. The parties will work in good faith to resolve disputes prior to escalation.
